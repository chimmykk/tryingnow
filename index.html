Welcome to Foxy Fam

Foxy left his home in the burrows chasing butterflies one day. Losing track of time, the day got darker and his trail back to safety was lost. Join in the journey as Foxy meets new friends in different regions on an adventure back to his family. He may encounter the rainbow clan, or the mysterious golden bandits. Help us in this community choose-your-own-adventure!

Each Foxy plays a role in this fun adventure. A collection made up of dozens of attributes brings personality and cuteness to each one!
